Through my innovative projects and research, I aim to make a positive impact on the environment.

Project

Flooding Mitigation with NBS(Natural Based Solutions)

Flooding has become a persistent issue in the area due to the inadequate height of the levees. Every time it rains, water overflows the levees and floods nearby villages. While the authorities have decided not to build higher levees because of the relatively small population and the perception that flood-related losses are limited, local residents continue to suffer significant disruptions. Additionally, farmlands in the region are frequently affected by flooding, leading to crop damage and financial losses for the farmers.

Flooding Mitigation in Wufang Village
